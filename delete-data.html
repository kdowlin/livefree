Delete your data

This page explains how to request deletion of your data from Live Free, the recovery app operated by Accountable, Inc.

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Live Free is built to be private by design. It has no account, and the recovery information you create lives only on your own phone and your personal cloud backup — it is never sent to or stored by Accountable, Inc. The only information that reaches our servers is a limited set of analytics and diagnostic data, described below.

How to request deletion

1. On-device data — delete it yourself, instantly

Uninstall the Live Free app from your phone. Everything Live Free stored on the device — your recovery path, daily goal, your “reasons” (text and photos), check-ins, pledges, and streaks — is removed with it. No request to us is needed.

To also remove the encrypted backup copy held in your own cloud account:

  • iPhone (iCloud): Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage, or turn iCloud off for Live Free.
  • Android (Google Drive backup): Google Drive app → Backups, or Settings → Google → Backup, and delete the Live Free backup.

2. Data on our servers — email us to delete it

  1. Email us at livefree@youareaccountable.com Use the same email address you entered in the Live Free app, if you provided one.
  2. Put “Delete my data” in the subject line If you didn’t share an email in the app, include any name or details you gave us so we can locate your records.
  3. We confirm and delete We’ll verify the request and permanently delete your data from our analytics systems, then email you to confirm.
Email livefree@youareaccountable.com

What data is deleted, and what is kept

The table below covers the limited data Live Free sends to our servers (via our analytics processor, PostHog). On-device data is not listed because it never reaches us — it is deleted when you uninstall the app (see step 1).

Email address and name (if you provided them)Deleted
Push notification token (if you enabled reminders)Deleted
Anonymous app-usage and diagnostic data — screens opened, taps, app performance, crash and error logs, and session recordings (with all typed text hidden)Deleted
Records we are legally required to retain (e.g. for tax, security, or fraud-prevention), if anyKept only as required by law, then deleted

We never collect your health information

By design, Live Free does not send us any health or recovery details — never your sober date, the substance or focus of your recovery, your streak length, or your precise location or address. There is no such data on our servers to delete.

Retention period

  • On-device data: deleted immediately when you uninstall the app; the personal cloud backup is removed when you delete it from your iCloud or Google account.
  • Server data: deleted from our active analytics systems within 30 days of a verified request, and purged from routine backups shortly after.
  • Legal holds: a small subset of records may be retained longer only where required by law or to prevent fraud and abuse, and is deleted once that purpose ends.

Questions

If you have any questions about deleting your data, contact Accountable, Inc. at livefree@youareaccountable.com.
